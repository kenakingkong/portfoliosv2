## Instructions

### Part 1: Preparing photos
1. Print photos with [this photo template]().
2. Cut out photos.

### Part 2: Attaching photos to matchboxes
1. Apply a thin layer of modge podge to the matchbox face
2. Place photo flat onto matchbox, gently smooth out air bubbles and wrinkles with your fingers.
3. Apply a another thin layer of modge podge on top of photo, sealing onto the matchbox.
4. Let it dry for 20 minutes or until it's no longer tacky.
